The "Invisibility" Crisis

Career gaps are often treated as liabilities, not transitions. Women returning to work face:

  • Algorithmic Bias: Standard portals filter out "non-linear" trajectories.
  • The Motherhood Penalty: Competence perception drops after a break.
  • Decision Fatigue: Lack of clear, actionable roadmaps.
  • Language Barriers: Exclusion of skilled non-English speakers.
